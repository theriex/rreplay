Imagine your watching one of those cool, slightly grainy sixties movies, the part where they go into the club with the great music coming from the band in the background, just loud enough to set the scene, but everyone can still hear each other's lines. Update that to today, with Eric laying down amazing glitch funk basslines and beats and Rich messing with the beats and mangling his guitars through a laptop, and you've got the gist of rreplay. Experimental and interesting to listen or move to, but also great ambient music that complements what people are doing rather than dominating it. You will want to rreplay it again and again.
