RREPlay pushes the envelope of analog digital music, driving close to and sometimes over the edge of system failure to carry evolving sound where it wants to go. The process is experimental, forming a unique interaction best described as glitch-jam. Informed by post-punk sensibilities, RREPlay ranges from ambient to alarming, from ice to surf, looping and dubbing along the way.
